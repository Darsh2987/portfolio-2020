About Me

Hi, I'm Darsh, I am from London, UK and I am a self taught Front-End Web Developer.

Boredom is my kryptonite and so therefore I keep myself productively busy or find something entertaining to do.

From the moment I built my first website no matter how basic or simple it was, I was amazed at what I had created. It was fascinating to learn just how a website works with the power of code, then again this was just by only using HTML and CSS. My interest was peaked, and I was hungry to learn more, however, this was nothing compared to when I wrote my first couple of lines of JavaScript, my mind was blown.

I spend the majority of my time learning and practicing to write code. It's exciting to build and learn something new. Learn to build new and different programming concepts. Work on a project from start to finish, be able to see the CSS apply the styling to the markup, see the functionality in action and then see the end result.

Building an app or website can sometimes be very challenging, especially when it comes to JavaScript, I have come across many challenges and there is no feeling like any other when overcoming a JavaScript challenge.

When I'm not coding I'll either be at the gym, playing/streaming some of my favorite video games, watching a movie or a TV serial, or spending time with my family or friends. I am a bit of a gym nut, I have always desired to be fit and have a great physique, but more importantly the intense training helps to relieve me of stress and keep me focused and motivated on achieving my life/career goals.
